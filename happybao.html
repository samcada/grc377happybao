Who We Are

happy bao. is a Taiwanese restaurant centered on family-style dining that brings people together through their love for food. Based in Los Angeles, California, the happy bao. family has served the local community for 10 years, a go-to spot for the likes of Jason Momoa and Barack Obama! happy bao. is a place for anyone looking to eat hot Xiao Long Bao, Spicy Wontons, or drink our famous Lychee and Rock Melon Martini! happy bao. offers a menu with high-quality, affordable food, a theme we share in the culture within our staff and service.
